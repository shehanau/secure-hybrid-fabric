## ITIL Service Management

### Incident Management

Restore services as quickly as possible following service disruptions.

### Problem Management

Identify root causes and implement permanent resolutions.

### Change Management

Control risk through structured change processes.

### Knowledge Management

Maintain operational documentation and knowledge sharing.

### Service Continuity

Support business resilience through disaster recovery planning.

### Continual Improvement

Review, measure, and improve services on an ongoing basis.
