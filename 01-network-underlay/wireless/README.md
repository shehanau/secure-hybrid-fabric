### Coverage

Provide consistent signal coverage throughout operational areas.

### Capacity

Support expected client density and future growth.

### Roaming

Enable seamless movement between access points.

### Security

Protect wireless access through modern authentication and encryption standards.

### Scalability

Support expansion without redesigning the wireless architecture.
